About Us

Welcome to Bookit. A haven for book lovers with comfy reading nooks and a curated selection of new and used books. Bookit was founded in 2019 and is located in Ogbomosho, Oyo state. They specialize in all genres of books, and sell at reasonable prices throughout Nigeria.
